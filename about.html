HISTORY PAINTING

Painting is the art of painting and drawing. Generally, an artist who makes a painting will express his ideas and feelings
about his painting on the surface of the canvas. In the process of making works of art, an artist needs creative
ideas.That's what makes works of art can be bought and sold at fantastic prices. The price of a painting is
usually seen based on the sale value and quality of the painting.

Painting is a branch of fine arts that is manifested through two-dimensional works using canvas or other flat surfaces.
The medium for painting is usually filled with the main elements of lines and colors resulting from the game of
cats or coloring and other image embellishments. The results of painting can contain representations
related to nature such as portraits of humans, animals, plants, and even landscapes.

Painting can also depict abstract images which are simplifications of natural forms. There are various definitions of
painting, according to Soedarso Sp, painting is an activity of processing a two-dimensional medium or a flat
surface of a three-dimensional object with the aim of getting a certain impression. Soedarso also added
that works of art are closely related to the full involvement of the creator's expressions, emotions
and ideas.
